Blogging with blogdown

Feb 11, 2018 #R #blogdown

So I’ve upgraded from the old and outdated ad-riddled wordpress up to a nice hugo website generated by blogdown. You can have a look at my old wordpress here. In my opinion the new blog is a huge improvement and much more efficient too. Here I’ll give some of the basics on how to make a similar one.

Disclaimer:

By no means am I an expert I’m just summarising some of the troubles I had. Some of the solutions I suggest here are probably not the right way to do things but they do work.

So why did I update? I always intended to do blog posts on data analysis methods and using blogdown is hugely advtantagous for this kind of thing. For example if I wanted to plot something in R then put it on my wordpress I’d have to plot it in R, save it with an appropriate size, find it in my folder and upload it to wordpress. Rmd it’s simiply:


```r
#eval=FALSE just means don't run this code

library(ggplot2)
qplot(cars$dist, cars$speed, xlab="Distance", ylab="Speed", main="Speed & Stopping distances of cars in 1920")
```
